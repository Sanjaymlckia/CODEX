Title: Integration Rules - Zoho CRM / Books / FODE

Rule 1 - FODE operational truth
FODE portal/admin/sheets remain the operational source of truth.
CRM must not take over lifecycle control for admissions processing.

Rule 2 - CRM role
Zoho CRM acts as commercial pipeline, relationship, and visibility layer.

Rule 3 - Books creation boundary
Zoho Books customer creation must occur at a deliberate, approved trigger point, not merely on raw lead capture.

Rule 4 - No destructive migration by assumption
Do not bulk convert customer identity models without explicit migration logic and approval.

Rule 5 - Preserve import evidence
Original import files, mapping files, and generated outputs must be preserved unchanged.

Rule 6 - Numbering discipline
Any customer/student numbering automation must follow the approved institutional numbering convention and be tied to a stable lifecycle event.

Rule 7 - Parent / sponsor handling
If Parent/Sponsor becomes the Books customer, student identity must still remain visible and reportable in CRM and finance workflows.

Rule 8 - Codex operating posture
Codex should prefer:

* inventory
* mapping
* rule clarification
* low-risk setup
  before any live CRM mutation work.
